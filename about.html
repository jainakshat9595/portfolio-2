Akshat Jain - About Me

I am a Digital Designer based in New Delhi, India. My passion is to craft beautiful, minimalist and useful designs. My philosophy is based on a simple guideline: everything should be made as simple as possible but not simpler; the extravagant should never obscure the essentials. I love to create visual languages which people will connect to easily, and finding the straightest path to go from A to B.

Over the years, I have gained a lot of experience by working with multiple parties, and the overall experience has allowed me to enhance my visual culture considerably. I believe that everything around us inspires what we do - this is why I try to explore and expand my reach as much as I can.
